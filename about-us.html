Our Story

More Than
Manufacturing

Hanim Botanical Pvt. Ltd. is an integrated Soil-to-Oil manufacturing company dedicated exclusively to one of the world's most rare and luxurious botanicals — Michelia champaca (Golden Champa).

Unlike conventional traders or aggregators, we control the entire value chain — from nurturing saplings in fertile soil to delivering meticulously extracted, premium-grade oils and absolutes to global markets.

"Every drop we produce carries traceability, purity, and purpose."

We are not just cultivating flowers. We are cultivating impact.

Economic Impact

A Farmer-First Model
That Creates Lasting Change

Spread across 110 acres of managed cultivation, our operations are built around a strong rural partnership model that transforms livelihoods.

High-Yield Saplings

We provide high-yield Champaca saplings to local farmers at no cost, ensuring quality from the source.

Guaranteed Buy-Back

Our guaranteed buy-back policy ensures farmers have a secure market and predictable income.

Perennial Cash Crop

Farmers transition from low-income seasonal crops to a high-value perennial cash crop with stable returns.

This shift transforms livelihoods. Farmers move from uncertainty to stability, from fluctuating incomes to predictable growth.

At Hanim Botanical, ethical sourcing is not a marketing claim — it is the foundation of our business.

Our Vision

To position India as a global leader in premium Champaca extracts

While creating a sustainable, farmer-driven ecosystem built on transparency, technology, and trust — ensuring every stakeholder benefits from botanical excellence.
